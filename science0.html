The Science

Nerve Grafts
MSCs
×

Nerve Grafts aim to minimize immune rejection. Autologous grafts use your own nerve tissue to integrate into the brain and reduce inflammation. Co-transplanting T cells helps reduce “needle trauma” inflammation. Clinical trials are ongoing.

×

MSCs (Mesenchymal stem cells) promote repair, reduce inflammation, and secrete protective factors. They’re under clinical trial as neuroprotective agents in Parkinson’s but don't yet replace dopamine neurons directly.
