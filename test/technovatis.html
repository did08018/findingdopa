Who We Are

Founded in 2007, Technovatis is a premier engineering firm based in Wavrin, France. We specialize in comprehensive technical studies, engineering consulting, and the conception of highly specialized machinery.

Our agile team of experts brings an engineering-first mindset to solving complex industrial challenges, specifically within the automotive and plastics industries. We take pride in holding patents that push the boundaries of industrial sorting techniques.
